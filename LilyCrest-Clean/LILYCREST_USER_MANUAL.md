# LilyCrest User Manual

Simple guide for tenants and testers

## 1. What LilyCrest is
LilyCrest is a mobile app for tenants. You can use it to:

- sign in securely
- check announcements
- view and pay bills
- send maintenance requests
- upload and download documents
- update your profile
- chat with Lily Assistant

## 2. Before You Start
You need:

- an Android phone
- the `app-release.apk` file
- an internet connection
- a valid tenant account
- access to your email for the login code, if asked

Important for testers:

- This APK works only while the LilyCrest test server is online.
- If the app opens but cannot load data, contact the person who sent you the APK.

## 3. How to Install the APK
1. Download the APK file to your Android phone.
2. Tap the APK file.
3. If your phone asks for permission, allow `Install unknown apps`.
4. Tap `Install`.
5. Open `LilyCrest`.

## 4. First Time Opening the App
When you open the app for the first time:

1. Read the onboarding slides.
2. Tap `Get Started` or `Next`.
3. You will be taken to the login screen.

## 5. How to Log In
### Option A: Email and Password
1. Enter your email address.
2. Enter your password.
3. Tap `Sign In`.
4. If asked, check your email for a 6-digit code.
5. Enter the code and tap `Verify & Log In`.

### Option B: Google Sign-In
1. Tap `Continue with Google`.
2. Choose your Google account.
3. Wait for the app to finish signing you in.

### Option C: Biometrics
You can use biometrics after enabling it.

1. Go to `Profile`.
2. Open `Settings`.
3. Turn on `Biometrics Login`.
4. Sign in once using your email and password.
5. Next time, tap `Sign in with Biometrics`.

## 6. Main Navigation
At the bottom of the app, you will see these main sections:

### `Services`
Use this to submit and manage maintenance requests.

### `Announcements`
Use this to read notices, reminders, and urgent updates from management.

### `Home`
Use this as your main dashboard.

### `Billings`
Use this to view bills, pay online, and download PDFs.

### `Profile`
Use this to update your account, open documents, access settings, and sign out.

## 7. Home Screen
The `Home` screen shows the most important information in one place:

- your room and tenancy details
- move-in and contract end dates
- billing summary
- maintenance summary
- latest notifications
- search bar for bills, maintenance, and policies
- Lily Assistant floating button

Use the search bar if you want to find something quickly.

## 8. How to Check Announcements
1. Tap `Announcements`.
2. Scroll to read all notices.
3. Tap any announcement to open the full message.
4. Use the category filters if needed.
5. Turn on the `Urgent` filter if you only want important notices.

## 9. How to View and Pay Bills
### To see your bills
1. Tap `Billings`.
2. Review your total outstanding amount.
3. Use the filters: `All`, `Pending`, `Overdue`, or `Paid`.
4. Tap any bill to open full details.

### To pay a bill
1. Open a bill from `Billings`.
2. Tap `Pay` or `Pay Now`.
3. Review the amount.
4. Tap `Pay via PayMongo`.
5. Complete the payment in the secure checkout page.

Available payment methods may include:

- GCash
- Maya
- credit/debit card
- online banking

### To download a bill or receipt
1. Open the bill.
2. Tap `PDF` or `Download Official Receipt`.
3. Wait while the file is prepared.

## 10. How to Submit a Maintenance Request
1. Tap `Services`.
2. Tap the request area to create a new request.
3. Choose the service type.
4. Choose the urgency level.
5. Describe the problem clearly.
6. Add photos if needed.
7. Tap `Submit Request`.

Service types include:

- Maintenance
- Plumbing
- Electrical
- Air Conditioning
- Cleaning
- Pest Control
- Furniture
- Other

Urgency levels:

- `Low`
- `Normal`
- `Urgent`

You can also:

- open a request to see details
- edit a pending request
- cancel a pending request
- reopen a resolved request
- submit a similar request again

## 11. How to Use Lily Assistant
The Lily Assistant button appears in the app as a floating helper button.

You can ask Lily about:

- billing
- maintenance
- documents
- house rules
- penalties
- account support

If Lily cannot fully answer your concern, Lily can transfer you to an admin.

### To contact an admin through Lily
1. Open `Lily Assistant`.
2. Type your concern.
3. If needed, ask to connect to admin.
4. Your inquiry will appear in the `Inquiries` section inside Lily Assistant.
5. Wait for the admin reply and continue the conversation there.

## 12. How to Use Documents
1. Go to `Profile`.
2. Tap `Documents`.

From there you can:

- view dormitory policy documents
- download your lease or policy PDFs
- upload personal documents
- preview uploaded files
- delete uploaded files if needed

Examples of upload types:

- Government ID
- Passport
- Driver's License
- Student ID
- Company ID
- Lease Extension
- Proof of Income
- Authorization Letter
- Other Document

## 13. How to Edit Your Profile
1. Go to `Profile`.
2. Tap `Edit Profile`.
3. Update your details.
4. Tap `Save Changes`.

You can update:

- full name
- username
- email
- phone number
- address
- profile picture

## 14. How to Use Settings
1. Go to `Profile`.
2. Tap `Settings`.

Inside Settings, you can:

- turn `Dark Mode` on or off
- turn `Push Notifications` on or off
- enable or disable `Biometrics Login`
- change your password
- open the Privacy Policy
- open the Terms of Service
- open the House Rules

## 15. How to Change Your Password
1. Go to `Profile`.
2. Tap `Settings`.
3. Tap `Change Password`.
4. Enter your new password.
5. Save the change.

If you forgot your password:

1. Go to the login screen.
2. Tap `Forgot password?`
3. Follow the reset instructions.

## 16. Quick Troubleshooting
### I cannot log in
- Check your email and password.
- Make sure you are using the correct tenant account.
- If a code is required, check your email inbox and spam folder.

### I did not receive the 6-digit code
- Wait a moment.
- Check spam or junk mail.
- Use `Resend Code` after the timer finishes.

### Bills or announcements are not loading
- Check your internet connection.
- Pull down to refresh.
- If the problem continues, the test server may be offline.

### Payment did not finish
- Return to the bill screen and check the payment status.
- If needed, wait a little and refresh.
- Do not pay again immediately unless the bill still shows unpaid.

### Biometrics is not working
- Make sure biometrics is enabled in your phone settings.
- Make sure biometrics is enabled in LilyCrest settings.
- Sign in once with email and password after turning biometrics on.

## 17. Best Practices for Users
- Keep your email address active because login codes may be sent there.
- Check announcements regularly.
- Pay bills before the due date.
- Add photos when sending maintenance requests.
- Keep your profile information updated.

## 18. When to Contact Admin
Contact the admin if:

- you cannot log in even with the correct details
- your bill looks wrong
- your payment is not reflected after waiting
- your maintenance issue is urgent
- you need help with documents or account access

## 19. One-Minute Summary
If you only remember the basics, remember this:

1. Install the APK.
2. Log in with your tenant account.
3. Use `Home` for overview.
4. Use `Billings` to pay.
5. Use `Services` for repairs.
6. Use `Announcements` for updates.
7. Use `Profile` for documents and settings.
8. Use `Lily Assistant` when you need help.
